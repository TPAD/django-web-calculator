Usage:
Calculator follows behavior in tables below. Division by zero is undefined and will reset the state if attempted.
You must enter a button before an operation, and you must enter a new number after pressing equals. You cannot continue operation with result.
Operations are interpreted as they are encountered, i.e. there is no operator precedence.
